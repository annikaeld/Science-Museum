Journey to the Stars: Unveiling the Secrets of the Universe!

Step into our awesome exhibit, 'The Sky Above Us,' and get ready to discover the secrets of the universe.

We're going to unveil the dazzling constellations and galaxies that light up our night sky, and you'll even learn about the clever ancient navigators who used them for their epic journeys.

Travel through time with us on our solar system quest! Join the fearless Arab traders, peek through Galileo's magical telescope, and zoom to the present day as we uncover the mysteries of the planets in our cosmic neighborhood. Get ready to blast off into an interstellar adventure like never before!
