Bio-Wonders: Discovering the Secrets of Life and Medicine!

From micro-organisms to the human body, groundbreaking discoveries in biology are providing us with unique insights into the wonders of the microscopic world.

Envision a world where people lead longer and healthier lives, all thanks to the brilliant minds of scientists and their remarkable medicines!

We'll guide you on a journey through time, exploring the fascinating history of medicine. You'll have the opportunity to meet heroes like the discoverer of penicillin and the pioneers behind the first heart transplant!

Many of our significant medicinal breakthroughs have not only originated in the lab but also from observing animals in the wild. Quite often, our fellow creatures have beaten us to the punch.
